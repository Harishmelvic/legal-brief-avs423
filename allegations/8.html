Allegation #8 of 15

Signed Contract Copy Not Returned

Medium Severity Strong Defence

Defence Story

The client's claim that he was never given a signed copy of the contract is disproven by the client's own action. On 22 May 2025, the client himself scanned the signed contract and uploaded it to the WhatsApp group as 'Final Contract AVS 423.pdf' (Line 158). He could only have scanned and uploaded this document if he had the physical original in his possession. The uploaded PDF contains both parties' signatures on all 12 pages, plus the client's father M. Vasanthan's witness signature on Page 12. One of the company directors has confirmed that the original signed copy was handed to the client on the day of signing. When Krithika later said 'The original copy of the agreement has to be with us' (Line 716), she was referring to the contractor's own copy for their records — not the client's copy, which had already been given to him months earlier. The client had the signed contract in his hands in May 2025, scanned it, and shared it. His claim in November 2025 that he still had not received it contradicts his own documented action.

Defence & Counter-Evidence

Contractor's Defence

1. The Original Was Given to the Client

The original signed contract was handed over to the client. The client scanned it and uploaded the scanned copy to the WhatsApp group himself:

22 May 2025 — Chat Line 158
Client (Mr Perinbanathan)
"Final Contract AVS 423.pdf (file attached)"

The client uploaded the scanned PDF of the signed contract himself. He could only have scanned it if he had the physical original in his possession.

2. The Contract Bears Both Signatures on All 12 Pages

The "Final Contract AVS 423.pdf" uploaded by the client contains both parties' signatures on every page (all 12 pages), plus the client's father M. Vasanthan's signature as witness on Page 12. This is the signed original that the client scanned and shared.

3. Director Has Confirmed Handover

One of the company directors has already confirmed that the original signed copy was handed over to the client. This can be presented as witness testimony if required.

4. Krithika's Message Was About the Contractor's Copy

When Krithika said "The original copy of the agreement has to be with us" (Line 716), she was referring to the contractor's own copy for their records. The client had already received and scanned his original copy months earlier (May 2025).

Chat Line 716
Krithika (Team)
"The original copy of the agreement has to be with us, and the soft copy with my signature is already available in the group."
Risk Assessment

Low Risk

The client's own action of scanning and uploading "Final Contract AVS 423.pdf" on 22 May 2025 proves he had the physical original in his possession. The contract bears both signatures on all 12 pages plus the father's witness signature. A director has confirmed the handover. The client's later claim of not receiving the signed copy is contradicted by his own upload of the scanned document.
