Allegation 14

Building Height Was Always 11 Feet

Severity: Medium Defence: Strong

Client's Exact Words

14 November 2025 · Line 671
Client
"No Harish, the original requirement was 11 feet of height. This was a mistake in the quotation, which was corrected in the contract."

Defence Points

Quotation said 10 ft: The original quotation specified a 10 ft ceiling height. This is the baseline from which all changes must be measured and is the starting point for cost estimation.
Client requested 11 ft: On 17 March, the client asked: "please check if there's a possibility to increase the clear height of the rooms about 11 ft as much as possible. If not, let's keep it at least 10.5 FT" (Line 22). This was a change request initiated by the client, not an original specification.
17 March 2025 · Line 22
Client
"please check if there's a possibility to increase the clear height of the rooms about 11 ft as much as possible. If not, let's keep it at least 10.5 FT"
Harish noted the change: "The building height is also changed to 10ft to 11ft from the quotation in the contract." (Line 669). This is an acknowledged scope change that affects cost. The contractor specifically flagged the discrepancy between quotation and contract.
14 November 2025 · Line 669
Harish (Contractor)
"The building height is also changed to 10ft to 11ft from the quotation in the contract."
Contract reflects 11 ft: The final contract includes 11 ft, which means both parties agreed to the change. The dispute is whether it was an additional cost item or included in the base price. The chat evidence clearly shows this was a client-driven modification from the original 10 ft specification.

Risk Assessment

Low Risk

The chat clearly shows the height was increased from 10 ft to 11 ft at the client's request. The contractor has documentation that this was a client-driven change from the original quotation. The client's claim that 11 ft was "always" the requirement is directly contradicted by the chronological chat evidence showing the change request on 17 March and the original 10 ft quotation.
